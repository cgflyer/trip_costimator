Total Travel Time

Estimated door-to-door time based on typical cruise TAS, trip distance, and any fuel-stop time.

Total Flight Time

Estimated flying time based on typical cruise TAS and trip distance - assuming no-winds and no delays.

Total Fuel Burn

Estimated fuel required using the profile's burn rate multiplied by the calculated flight time with taxi and takeoff fuel added.

Total Cost

Approximate member cost including hourly rate, estimated tach hours, daily minimums, and sales tax.

Daily Minimum Impact

For multi-day trips, the tool applies the aircraft's daily minimum if it exceeds the calculated flight time.

Refueling stops

Passenger and baggage weight and fuel capacity with reserves may require a fuel stop for long distance.

Fuel adjustments

If you expect a refueling price on the trip, the cost difference from base reimbursement rate may add or subtract from total cost.

>
How Cost Factors Are Applied
  • Hourly rate — multiplied by estimated or minimum time.
  • Hourly cost - influenced by profile RPM setting vs standard 2400 RPM
  • Fuel burn x fuel price — adjusted for club reimbursement or surcharge policy.
  • Daily minimums — applied when the aircraft is away overnight.
Purpose of These Outputs

These values show the relative differences between aircraft so members can choose the most suitable airplane before detailed planning.
